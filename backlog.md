
# Product Backlog
## Barangay Health Center Patient Record and Appointment Management System

### Project Goal
Develop a web-based Barangay Health Center Patient Record and Appointment Management System that allows healthcare staff to efficiently manage patient records, appointments, medical histories, and health services.

---

## User Story 1 – Patient Management (CRUD)
**As a** health center staff member,
**I want** to manage patient records,
**So that** patient information is organized and easy to access.

### Tasks
- Create a new patient record.
- View all patient records.
- Update patient information.
- Delete patient records.
- Search patient by name or ID.

**Priority:** High

---

## User Story 2 – Appointment Management (CRUD)
**As a** health center staff member,
**I want** to manage patient appointments,
**So that** consultations are properly scheduled.

### Tasks
- Create appointment.
- View appointment list.
- Update appointment schedule.
- Cancel/Delete appointment.
- Search appointments by patient or date.

**Priority:** High

---

## User Story 3 – Medical Records (CRUD)
**As a** healthcare worker,
**I want** to manage medical records,
**So that** patient consultation history is properly stored.

### Tasks
- Add medical record.
- View medical history.
- Update diagnosis and treatment.
- Delete medical record.

**Priority:** High

---

## User Story 4 – Health Services Management (CRUD)
**As a** health center administrator,
**I want** to manage available health services,
**So that** services offered by the health center are updated.

### Tasks
- Add health service.
- View health services.
- Update service information.
- Delete health service.

**Priority:** Medium

---

## User Story 5 – User Management (CRUD)
**As an** administrator,
**I want** to manage system users,
**So that** only authorized personnel can access the system.

### Tasks
- Create staff account.
- View registered users.
- Update user information.
- Delete user account.
- Assign user roles (Administrator/Staff).

**Priority:** Medium

# Acceptance Criteria

## Patient Management
- Staff can add, edit, delete, and view patients.
- Patient information is stored correctly.

## Appointment Management
- Staff can create and manage appointments.
- Appointment schedules are displayed correctly.

## Medical Records
- Medical history is linked to each patient.
- Diagnosis and treatment can be updated.

## Health Services
- Services can be added, updated, and removed.

## User Management
- Only administrators can manage user accounts.
- Staff can securely log in.
